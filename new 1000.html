About ActiveNYP


Active NYP is the logo of the website.
This website is to encourange poly students to spend their spare time to plan out activities.
It is also to train their leadership skills so that they can lead their participants in the event.
This website wil serve the needs of the students who want to improve their leadership skills and communication skills.
So what are you waiting for? Go participate events made by NYP students and grab your friends along to.
Last, people who plan out the activities can find their friends to participate with you.


"Serving others prepares you to lead others" . Start leading people and create more meaningful memories.
